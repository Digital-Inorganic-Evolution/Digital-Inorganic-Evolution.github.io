Shallow Project

By drawing inspiration from the evolution of organic life, we conduct an experiment in Digital Inorganic Evolution. The objective is to create a digital inorganic entity the "Shallow Project" capable of surviving within the Hong Kong horse racing environment. Survival is defined as achieving a sustained positive net gain over the long term, interpreted simply as "winning", the energy rewarded must be larger than the energy consumed.

Hong Kong Horse Racing Predictions and Comparison

The survival comparison assumes an energy consumption per hunting of HKD $10 each on win and place bets for the highest ranked predicted horse in every race, beginning on 1 February 2026.
